# AI Product Engineering Program
## High-Impact AI MVPs for Critical Sectors

This framework focuses on **building AI products that solve real structural problems** in critical sectors:

- Health
- Education
- Security
- Agriculture
- Internet Infrastructure

The engineering philosophy is:

1. Build **small AI MVPs**
2. Test them in real environments
3. Improve engineering architecture
4. Scale infrastructure gradually
5. Turn products into **robust technology platforms**

As a **technical instructor**, the process prioritizes:

- deep engineering understanding
- system-level thinking
- teaching through product building
- exposing engineers to real-world technical complexity

The emphasis is **engineering-first AI product development**, not theoretical AI.

---

# Product Development Philosophy

All AI products follow the same engineering progression.

```

Problem Identification
↓
Engineering MVP
↓
Real User Testing
↓
Architecture Improvement
↓
Infrastructure Scaling
↓
Platform-Level Product

```

Each stage is used as a **teaching module for engineers**.

Students learn by **building actual systems**.
